In a plasma -only donation,the liquid portion of the donor's blood is drawn from one are and sent through a high-tech machine that collects the plasma.The donor's red blood cells and platelets are then returned to donor along with some saline.

Benefits of donating plasma

  • Lower Blood Pressure
  • Improved Immunity
  • Better Sleep Quality
